My Top 3 Comedy Destinations

  1. YouTube

    This is my go to because of the endless supply of stand-up clips and sketches. Whether it's old SNL highlights or new creators, there's always something to keep me laughing.

  2. The Onion

    I love this site for its razor sharp satire. They write fake news stories that look so real it makes the actual headlines even funnier (and sometimes scarier!).

  3. Reddit (r/funny)

    It's a huge community where people share memes and funny videos from all over the world. It's the best place for a quick "scroll and chuckle" during a break.
